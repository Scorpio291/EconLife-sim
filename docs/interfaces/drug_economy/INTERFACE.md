# Module: drug_economy

## Purpose
Processes the drug supply chain each tick: drug production at criminal facilities, wholesale and retail distribution through NPCBusiness records with criminal_sector == true, pricing through the RegionalMarket informal layer, quality/purity tracking through distribution tiers, and consumer demand from background population addiction rates. Supports four V1 drug types (cannabis, methamphetamine, synthetic_opioid, designer_drug) with province-specific legalization status determining whether a drug prices through the formal or informal market layer.

Province-parallel execution: each province's drug production, distribution, and pricing operates independently. Cross-province drug shipments use the existing TransitShipment infrastructure with interception checks per tick during transit.

## Inputs (from WorldState)
- `npc_businesses` — filtered to businesses with `criminal_sector == true` producing drug goods; iterated per province via `NPCBusiness.province_id`. Includes `drug_market_tier` (wholesale or retail) for pricing and counterpart selection.
- `regional_markets` — informal layer spot prices per (drug_good_id x province_id); used for revenue calculation and wholesale price derivation
- `provinces` — province list for province-parallel dispatch; includes `drug_legalization_status` map (DrugType -> bool) determining formal vs informal market layer per drug
- `significant_npcs` — criminal_operator and criminal_enforcer NPCs involved in distribution network
- `evidence_pool` — evidence tokens attributed to drug operations for investigator integration
- `current_tick` — for production scheduling, transit arrival timing, and seasonal effects
- `config.drug.wholesale_price_fraction` — default 0.45; wholesale price as fraction of retail spot price
- `deferred_work_queue` — pending TransitShipment entries for drug goods in transit between provinces

## Outputs (to DeltaBuffer)
- `MarketDelta.supply_delta` — additive supply contribution per (drug_good_id, province_id) from drug production output this tick via informal market layer
- `MarketDelta.demand_buffer_delta` — consumer demand for drugs from background population cohort addiction rates; derived demand for precursor chemicals consumed in production
- `BusinessDelta.cash_delta` — operating cost deduction for criminal facility production (precursor costs, labor); revenue credited from drug sales at informal spot_price (wholesale or retail)
- `BusinessDelta.revenue_delta` — revenue from drug distribution at applicable market tier price
- `BusinessDelta.output_quality` — purity/quality grade of production output; degrades multiplicatively per distribution tier
- `EvidenceTokenDelta` — physical and financial evidence tokens generated by drug production and distribution activities; attributed to operating actor or active alternative identity
- `CrossProvinceDelta` — drug shipment dispatches targeting other provinces via TransitShipment infrastructure

## Preconditions
- `deferred_work_queue` has been drained (Step 2 complete); transit arrivals for drug shipments this tick are already applied to informal market supply.
- Recipe registry for drug production is loaded and immutable (precursor -> drug_good mappings from packages/base_game/goods.csv).
- Province drug_legalization_status is current (may have changed via legislative cycle in previous ticks).
- Criminal operations module has completed; CriminalOrganization territory and dominance values are current.

## Postconditions
- Every active criminal NPCBusiness producing drugs has had its recipe executed exactly once for this tick.
- RegionalMarket informal layer supply for each drug good in each province reflects this tick's local production output.
- Consumer demand from addiction rates is written to demand_buffer_delta for the next tick's informal price calculation.
- Drug quality/purity degrades through each distribution tier: production -> wholesale -> retail. Quality value is tracked per batch.
- Revenue credited to criminal businesses at the appropriate market tier price (wholesale_price or retail spot_price).
- Legalized drugs (province.drug_legalization_status[type] == true) price through the formal market layer; criminalized drugs through the informal layer.
- Precursor chemical demand (derived demand) is recorded for supply chain propagation.

## Invariants
- DrugType enum: cannabis=0, methamphetamine=1, synthetic_opioid=2, designer_drug=3 (V1). cocaine=4, heroin=5 (EX, reserved).
- DrugMarketTier enum: wholesale=0, retail=1.
- Wholesale price formula: `wholesale_price(drug_type, province) = RegionalMarket.spot_price(good_key(drug_type), province, informal_layer) * config.drug.wholesale_price_fraction`.
- Wholesale businesses transact with other criminal businesses; retail businesses transact with background NPC population cohorts.
- Quality/purity degrades multiplicatively per distribution layer. Production output quality is capped by facility tech_tier and recipe constraints (same formula as production module).
- Methamphetamine production generates high chemical_waste_signature in FacilitySignals (primary detection surface).
- Synthetic opioid production may divert from pharmaceutical precursors, creating medical/criminal supply chain overlap and documentary evidence.
- Cannabis legal status varies by province (scenario seeded). Legal cannabis prices through formal market; illegal through informal.
- Designer drugs are legal until scheduled (see designer_drug module); while unscheduled they price through formal market with designer_legal_margin_mult.
- Criminal facility production runs through the same recipe execution logic as the production module but uses informal market prices.
- Floating-point accumulations use canonical sort order (good_id ascending, then province_id ascending) for deterministic summation.
- Same seed + same inputs = identical drug economy output regardless of core count.
- All random draws (interception checks, quality variance) through `DeterministicRNG`.

## Failure Modes
- Precursor chemical unavailable (supply exhausted): drug production clamped to zero for that recipe; output reduced proportionally. Bottleneck recorded for supply chain propagation.
- Drug legalization status missing for a DrugType in a province: default to illegal (informal market layer). Log warning.
- Criminal facility destroyed (raid consequence): production drops to zero; existing inventory in transit continues to destination.
- NaN or negative output from floating-point edge case: clamp to 0.0, log diagnostic.
- No active criminal businesses producing a drug type in a province: supply = 0; price rises toward ceiling; demand unmet, addiction rates may decay.

## Performance Contract
- Province-parallel execution across up to 6 provinces.
- Target: < 20ms total for all drug production and distribution across 6 provinces (~100-300 criminal businesses) on 6 cores.
- Per-business drug recipe execution: < 0.015ms average (same budget as production module).
- Must not exceed tick budget share that would push total tick above 200ms target.

## Dependencies
- runs_after: ["criminal_operations", "production"]
- runs_before: ["npc_spending", "addiction"]

## Test Scenarios
- `test_drug_production_consumes_precursors`: A methamphetamine lab with precursor inputs (pseudoephedrine + chemical_inputs). Verify correct precursor consumption from informal supply and methamphetamine added to informal supply.
- `test_wholesale_price_fraction_applied`: Retail spot_price for cannabis is 100. Verify wholesale price = 100 * 0.45 = 45 for wholesale-tier businesses.
- `test_retail_business_credits_at_spot_price`: Retail-tier drug business sells synthetic_opioid. Verify revenue calculation uses full informal spot_price, not wholesale fraction.
- `test_quality_degrades_through_distribution`: Drug produced at quality 0.95. Wholesale distribution degrades to quality * wholesale_degradation_factor. Retail further degrades. Verify multiplicative degradation chain.
- `test_legal_cannabis_uses_formal_market`: Province with drug_legalization_status[cannabis] = true. Verify cannabis production and sales price through formal RegionalMarket layer, not informal.
- `test_illegal_cannabis_uses_informal_market`: Province with drug_legalization_status[cannabis] = false. Verify cannabis uses informal market layer and generates appropriate evidence tokens.
- `test_consumer_demand_from_addiction_rate`: Province with high synthetic_opioid addiction rate in background population. Verify demand_buffer_delta for synthetic_opioid reflects addiction-driven consumer demand.
- `test_precursor_shortage_clamps_production`: Lab requires 10 units pseudoephedrine but only 3 available. Verify output clamped proportionally and no negative supply results.
- `test_cross_province_shipment_dispatched`: Drug business dispatches shipment to adjacent province. Verify TransitShipment created in deferred_work_queue with correct arrival_tick and interception_check entries.
- `test_interception_generates_evidence`: Drug shipment intercepted during transit. Verify physical EvidenceToken generated and supply does not arrive at destination.
- `test_province_parallel_determinism`: Run 50 ticks of drug economy with 6 provinces on 1 core and 6 cores. Verify bit-identical supply, demand, cash, and revenue deltas.
- `test_meth_chemical_waste_signal`: Methamphetamine lab in operation. Verify FacilitySignals.chemical_waste_signature reflects high chemical waste from meth production recipe.
