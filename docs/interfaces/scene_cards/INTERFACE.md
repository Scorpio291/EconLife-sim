# Module: scene_cards

## Purpose
Evaluates trigger conditions for pending scene cards, selects which cards to present to the player this tick, computes NPC presentation state from relationship and risk tolerance, and processes player choices into consequence deltas. Scene cards are the primary player interaction mechanism for NPC encounters, narrative events, and decision points.

## Inputs (from WorldState)
- `current_tick` — for evaluating time-based trigger conditions and calendar linkage
- `calendar` — calendar entries whose `start_tick == current_tick` trigger their linked `scene_card_id`
- `pending_scene_cards` — scene cards generated by other modules awaiting delivery
- `significant_npcs` — NPC state for dialogue generation, relationship score lookup, and `npc_presentation_state` derivation
- `player` — player state including current_province_id (physical presence requirement), relationships, knowledge map
- `regional_markets` — economic context for procedurally generated business meeting dialogue
- `npc_businesses` — business state for board dispute cards, acquisition negotiation cards, and business meeting context

## Outputs (to DeltaBuffer)
- `SceneCardDelta` — new scene cards added to `pending_scene_cards` for UI delivery (generated from calendar triggers and procedural generation)
- `NPCDelta.updated_relationship` — relationship changes from player choice consequences (trust changes, obligation creation)
- `NPCDelta.new_memory_entry` — NPC memory entries recording the interaction and player's choice
- `PlayerDelta` — player stat changes from choice consequences (wealth, health, skill, exhaustion)
- `EvidenceDelta` — evidence tokens generated by player choices (e.g., witnessed bribe, recorded threat)
- `ObligationDelta` — new obligation nodes created by player commitments during scene card choices
- Queued `ConsequenceEntry` — delayed consequences from player choices scheduled in DeferredWorkQueue

## Preconditions
- Calendar module has executed this tick (deadline-expired entries already processed; active calendar entries with `start_tick == current_tick` are identified).
- All `SceneCard.npc_id` values reference valid NPCs.
- Player `current_province_id` is set (physical presence determines which in-person scene cards can fire).
- Scene card template data (authored and procedural) is loaded from package content files.

## Postconditions
- All calendar-triggered scene cards for this tick are added to `pending_scene_cards`.
- `npc_presentation_state` is computed for each scene card from the NPC's relationship score with the player and current risk tolerance: 0.0 (hostile/closed) to 1.0 (open/cooperative).
- Player choices from previously pending scene cards have been resolved into consequence deltas.
- Scene cards that have been fully resolved (player made a choice, consequences applied) are removed from `pending_scene_cards`.
- No more than the configured maximum scene cards are queued for delivery in a single tick (prevents UI overload).

## Invariants
- `npc_presentation_state` is always derived from `npc.relationships[player].trust` and `npc.risk_tolerance`; it is never stored persistently or set manually.
- Scene cards require physical presence for in-person settings: settings like boardroom, restaurant, factory_floor require `player.current_province_id == scene_card.npc.province_id`. Remote settings (phone_call, video_call) have no province constraint.
- Authored scene cards take priority over procedurally generated cards when both trigger for the same NPC in the same tick.
- Player choices are final; no undo mechanism. Each choice maps to a consequence payload that executes deterministically.
- Scene card types are: meeting, call, personal_event, news_notification.
- Scene settings constrain available interactions: prison_cell limits to prison-available actions; open_plan_office limits conversation sensitivity.

## Failure Modes
- Scene card references invalid NPC (deleted or dead): skip card generation, log warning. If NPC died after card was queued but before delivery, card is discarded with no consequence.
- Player not in correct province for in-person scene: card remains in pending queue; not delivered until physical presence condition met or card expires.
- Missing authored scene card template: fall back to procedural generation for that interaction type, log warning.

## Performance Contract
- Sequential execution (not province-parallel).
- Target: < 10ms per tick for evaluating triggers and generating up to 5 scene cards.
- Procedural dialogue generation: < 2ms per card.
- Choice consequence resolution: < 1ms per resolved choice.

## Dependencies
- runs_after: ["calendar"]
- runs_before: []

## Test Scenarios
- `test_calendar_entry_triggers_scene_card`: Calendar entry with `start_tick = 10` and `scene_card_id = 42`. At tick 10, verify scene card 42 is added to `pending_scene_cards`.
- `test_npc_presentation_state_reflects_trust`: NPC with trust 0.8 toward player. Verify `npc_presentation_state` is in the high cooperative range (close to 1.0). Same NPC with trust 0.1, verify state is in the hostile/closed range (close to 0.0).
- `test_hostile_npc_different_from_cooperative`: Two scene cards with the same NPC at trust 0.2 vs trust 0.9. Verify `npc_presentation_state` values are meaningfully different, reflecting the visual feedback for relationship quality.
- `test_player_choice_applies_relationship_delta`: Player selects a choice that includes `relationship_delta = +0.10`. Verify NPC trust increases by 0.10 after choice resolution.
- `test_player_choice_generates_evidence_token`: Player selects a bribe choice. Verify an EvidenceToken is created with appropriate type and subject_id.
- `test_player_choice_creates_obligation`: Player promises a favor during a meeting. Verify an ObligationNode is created in the obligation network.
- `test_in_person_scene_requires_province_match`: Scene card with setting = restaurant for an NPC in province 3. Player is in province 1. Verify card is not delivered. Move player to province 3; verify card is delivered.
- `test_phone_call_ignores_province`: Scene card with setting = phone_call. Player in any province. Verify card is delivered regardless of province mismatch.
- `test_dead_npc_card_discarded`: NPC dies at tick 8. Scene card for that NPC queued at tick 7. At tick 9, verify card is discarded from pending queue with no consequences applied.
- `test_authored_card_takes_priority_over_procedural`: Both an authored and procedural scene card trigger for the same NPC at the same tick. Verify only the authored card is delivered.
- `test_prison_setting_limits_available_choices`: Scene card in prison_cell setting. Verify choice options are restricted to prison-available actions (no business operations, no physical meetings outside prison).
- `test_news_notification_has_no_npc_presentation`: Scene card of type news_notification. Verify no `npc_presentation_state` is computed (no NPC portrait interaction).
