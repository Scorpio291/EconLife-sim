# Module: designer_drug

## Purpose
Manages the designer drug scheduling race lifecycle each tick: tracks DesignerDrugRecord status through the unscheduled -> review_initiated -> scheduled pipeline, runs monthly detection checks against cumulative evidence visible to regulators, processes the SchedulingProcess review duration (scaled by political delay factor), executes market layer transitions when scheduling is enacted, and manages the market margin dynamics that create the player's incentive to maintain a pipeline of successor compounds. The scheduling race is the core dynamic: a designer drug is legal and highly profitable until regulators detect it and schedule it, at which point the R&D pipeline must have a successor ready or a supply gap opens.

Not province-parallel: designer drug records are actor-level, scheduling processes operate at national level, and detection checks require aggregating evidence across the producing actor's full evidence profile.

## Inputs (from WorldState)
- `designer_drug_records` — all DesignerDrugRecord records: id, compound_key, producing_actor_id, scheduling_status (unscheduled/review_initiated/scheduled), synthesized_tick, detection_tick, scheduling_enacted_tick, market_margin_multiplier, rnd_project_id, scheduling_process_id
- `evidence_pool` — evidence tokens attributed to the producing actor; filtered to financial + physical types held by LE/regulator NPCs for detection threshold check
- `significant_npcs` — NPC records with role IN {law_enforcement, regulator} for evidence holder filtering in detection check
- `regional_markets` — market data for the compound's good_key entry; formal layer (while unscheduled) and informal layer (after scheduling)
- `global_technology_state` — GlobalTechnologyState containing active_scheduling_reviews (SchedulingProcess records) and CriminalRnDProject references
- `provinces` — province list for producing_actor province resolution
- `current_tick` — for detection timing, scheduling enactment timing, and review duration checks
- `config.drug.designer_detection_threshold` — default 2.5; cumulative evidence weight triggering scheduling review
- `config.drug.scheduling_review_duration` — default 180 ticks (~6 months); review duration in fast political system; scaled by political_delay_factor
- `config.drug.designer_legal_margin_mult` — default 2.5; price multiplier while compound is unscheduled (legal premium)
- `config.drug.designer_post_schedule_margin` — default 0.80; margin fraction when no successor in pipeline after scheduling

## Outputs (to DeltaBuffer)
- `DesignerDrugRecordDelta.scheduling_status` — transitions: unscheduled -> review_initiated (on detection), review_initiated -> scheduled (on enactment)
- `DesignerDrugRecordDelta.detection_tick` — set to current_tick when detection threshold crossed
- `DesignerDrugRecordDelta.scheduling_enacted_tick` — set to current_tick when scheduling enacted
- `DesignerDrugRecordDelta.market_margin_multiplier` — starts at designer_legal_margin_mult (2.5); falls to 1.0 on scheduling; falls further to designer_post_schedule_margin (0.80) if no successor compound
- `DesignerDrugRecordDelta.scheduling_process_id` — linked to newly created SchedulingProcess on detection
- `GlobalTechnologyStateDelta` — new SchedulingProcess record added to active_scheduling_reviews on detection; process.is_enacted set to true on enactment
- `MarketDelta.price_layer_transition` — compound's good_key moves from formal to informal RegionalMarket layer immediately on scheduling enactment
- `MarketDelta.supply_delta` — if no successor compound exists for the producing actor after scheduling, informal market supply for designer_drug drops to 0 for that actor

## Preconditions
- Evidence pool contains all tokens generated by preceding modules this tick (production, facility signals, investigator engine).
- Criminal R&D projects that completed this tick have created DesignerDrugRecord entries.
- RegionalMarket has entries for the compound's good_key (added when CriminalRnDProject completed).
- GlobalTechnologyState is accessible for SchedulingProcess record creation and tracking.

## Postconditions
- Monthly detection check (when current_tick aligns with monthly schedule) has evaluated all unscheduled DesignerDrugRecords against the evidence threshold.
- Detected compounds have scheduling_status = review_initiated and a SchedulingProcess created with review_duration scaled by political_delay_factor.
- Enacted scheduling processes (current_tick >= detection_tick + review_duration) have set scheduling_status = scheduled, transitioned the compound to informal market pricing, and evaluated successor compound availability.
- Market margin multiplier reflects current scheduling status and successor availability.
- All inventory of a scheduled compound in formal market is now criminal stock in informal market.

## Invariants
- SchedulingStatus enum: unscheduled=0, review_initiated=1, scheduled=2.
- Detection trigger: cumulative evidence_weight of financial + physical tokens attributed to producing_actor_id, held by LE/regulator NPCs, exceeds config.drug.designer_detection_threshold (2.5). Checked monthly.
- Detection evidence formula: `evidence_weight = sum(token.actionability for token in evidence_pool WHERE token.subject_npc_id == record.producing_actor_id AND token.type IN {financial, physical} AND token.holder_npc_id has role IN {law_enforcement, regulator})`.
- Scheduling review duration: `config.drug.scheduling_review_duration * political_delay_factor(province.nation_id)`. Slower political systems take longer to schedule.
- On scheduling enactment: all inventory of the compound moves from formal to informal market layer immediately. Price layer transition is instantaneous.
- Successor gap: if no DesignerDrugRecord WHERE producing_actor_id == record.producing_actor_id AND scheduling_status == unscheduled exists after scheduling, informal market supply for designer_drug drops to 0 for that actor. Market gap opportunity for rival producers.
- Market margin multiplier: 2.5 while unscheduled (legal premium), 1.0 on scheduling (same as other informal drugs), 0.80 if no successor compound (liquidation pressure).
- Compound key format: "designer_drug_{id}". Also serves as the goods.csv key for RegionalMarket entry.
- Same seed + same inputs = identical designer drug output (deterministic).
- All random draws (political_delay_factor variation) through `DeterministicRNG`.

## Failure Modes
- Producing actor dead or imprisoned: compound remains in market but no new production occurs. Existing inventory depletes. Scheduling process continues regardless. Log diagnostic.
- No LE/regulator NPC in province: detection check finds no evidence holders; compound remains unscheduled indefinitely. Log warning.
- CriminalRnDProject reference invalid (rnd_project_id points to nonexistent project): compound remains functional but R&D linkage broken. Log warning.
- Political delay factor returns 0 or negative: clamp review_duration to minimum 30 ticks. Log diagnostic.
- NaN or negative evidence_weight: clamp to 0.0, log diagnostic.

## Performance Contract
- Sequential execution (not province-parallel); designer drug records are actor-level and detection requires cross-province evidence aggregation.
- Target: < 5ms total for all designer drug processing (~5-20 records typical).
- Monthly detection check: < 10ms across all unscheduled compounds.
- Must not exceed tick budget share that would push total tick above 200ms target.

## Dependencies
- runs_after: ["investigator_engine", "drug_economy"]
- runs_before: ["legal_process"]

## Test Scenarios
- `test_detection_on_evidence_threshold`: Unscheduled compound with producing_actor evidence weight of 2.6 (above 2.5 threshold). Run monthly detection check. Verify scheduling_status transitions to review_initiated and SchedulingProcess created.
- `test_no_detection_below_threshold`: Unscheduled compound with evidence weight 1.8 (below threshold). Verify scheduling_status remains unscheduled.
- `test_scheduling_enacted_after_review_duration`: Compound at review_initiated with detection_tick = 100 and review_duration = 180. At tick 280, verify scheduling_status transitions to scheduled and scheduling_enacted_tick set.
- `test_political_delay_scales_review`: Same compound in two nations: one with political_delay_factor 1.0 and another with 2.0. Verify review_duration is 180 and 360 respectively.
- `test_formal_to_informal_market_transition`: Compound scheduled. Verify good_key moves from formal to informal RegionalMarket layer and all existing inventory is now criminal stock.
- `test_margin_drops_to_1_on_scheduling`: Compound with market_margin_multiplier 2.5. On scheduling, verify multiplier drops to 1.0.
- `test_margin_drops_further_without_successor`: Compound scheduled and no other unscheduled DesignerDrugRecord for same producing_actor. Verify margin drops to 0.80 (designer_post_schedule_margin).
- `test_successor_prevents_margin_collapse`: Compound scheduled but producing_actor has another unscheduled compound. Verify margin stays at 1.0 (no further drop).
- `test_supply_gap_on_no_successor`: Compound scheduled with no successor. Verify informal market supply for designer_drug = 0 for that actor. Market gap opportunity for rivals.
- `test_detection_only_counts_le_regulator_held_evidence`: Evidence tokens held by journalist NPCs (not LE/regulator). Verify these tokens do not count toward detection threshold.
- `test_monthly_detection_not_run_every_tick`: Verify detection check runs only on monthly schedule ticks, not every tick.
- `test_determinism_across_runs`: Run 200 ticks with multiple designer drug records using fixed seed. Verify bit-identical detection, scheduling, and market transition outcomes.
