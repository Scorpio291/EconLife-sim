# Module: investigator_engine

## Purpose
Processes all law enforcement, regulatory, and journalist NPC investigators each tick: reads facility signals, evidence tokens, and NPC knowledge to update InvestigatorMeter levels, derive investigator status thresholds, gather evidence, and advance case-building toward prosecution. Investigator types (law_enforcement, regulator, journalist, ngo_investigator) have distinct capabilities and target selection logic but share the same InvestigatorMeter infrastructure.

Province-parallel execution: each province's investigators operate independently on local facility signals and regional criminal activity. Cross-province investigation coordination is expansion scope; V1 investigators operate within their home province only.

## Inputs (from WorldState)
- `significant_npcs` — filtered to NPCs with `role IN {law_enforcement, regulator, journalist, ngo_investigator}` and `status == active`; iterated per province via `current_province_id`
- `npc_businesses` — all NPCBusiness records; filtered by `criminal_sector == true` for LE signal aggregation; filtered by all businesses for regulator scrutiny
- `evidence_pool` — all active EvidenceToken records; read to compute evidence weight per target and to feed InvestigatorMeter fill rate from FIU tokens
- `provinces` — province list for province-parallel dispatch
- `regional_markets` — read for revenue/traffic discrepancy detection (commingling signal for regulators)
- `current_tick` — for opened_tick assignment, stage duration checks, and decay timing
- `config.opsec.facility_count_normalizer` — default 5.0; divisor for regional signal aggregation
- `config.opsec.detection_to_fill_rate_scale` — default 0.005; converts net_signal to per-tick meter fill
- `config.opsec.fill_rate_max` — default 0.01; cap on per-tick meter fill
- `config.opsec.personnel_violence_multiplier` — default 3.0; fill_rate multiplier during violence
- `config.investigator.surveillance_threshold` — default 0.30; level at which surveillance begins
- `config.investigator.formal_inquiry_threshold` — default 0.60; level at which formal investigation opens
- `config.investigator.raid_threshold` — default 0.80; level at which raid is planned
- `config.investigator.warrant_trust_min` — default 0.30; minimum institutional_trust for warrant
- `config.investigator.decay_rate` — default 0.001; per-tick meter reduction when detection risk is zero

## Outputs (to DeltaBuffer)
- `InvestigatorMeterDelta.fill_rate` — updated fill rate per LE NPC derived from regional criminal facility signal, corruption coverage, and evidence token actionability
- `InvestigatorMeterDelta.current_level` — updated meter level (current_level + fill_rate, clamped to [0.0, 1.0])
- `InvestigatorMeterDelta.status` — derived status from threshold comparison (inactive, surveillance, formal_inquiry, raid_imminent)
- `InvestigatorMeterDelta.target_id` — resolved target from regional signal; argmax of known criminal actors' estimated signal contribution in province
- `EvidenceTokenDelta` — new physical EvidenceToken generated when status transitions to surveillance; new evidence tokens from formal inquiry warrant execution
- `ConsequenceDelta(investigation_opens)` — queued when status first reaches formal_inquiry
- `ConsequenceDelta(raid_imminent)` — queued when status reaches raid_imminent; delay 7-30 ticks (seed-deterministic)
- `NPCDelta.knowledge_map` — KnowledgeEntry additions for investigators who discover evidence tokens or activity knowledge during surveillance

## Preconditions
- `deferred_work_queue` has been drained (Step 2 complete); facility signal composites are current from tick step 12 (facility_signals).
- All `FacilitySignals.net_signal` values are computed for this tick.
- Criminal operations module has completed; criminal businesses and territorial conflict states are current.
- Evidence pool contains all tokens generated by preceding modules this tick.

## Postconditions
- Every active investigator NPC (law_enforcement, regulator, journalist, ngo_investigator) in every province has had its InvestigatorMeter evaluated exactly once for this tick.
- InvestigatorMeter.status is derived from current_level against named thresholds; no stale status values persist.
- New evidence tokens are generated for surveillance actions (physical type) and formal inquiry warrants (documentary or financial type).
- Formally opened investigations (status >= formal_inquiry) do not close on signal drop alone; require active intervention or meter decay below threshold over sustained ticks.
- Corruption coverage reduces fill_rate multiplicatively: `fill_rate *= (1.0 - corruption_susceptibility * regional_corruption_coverage)`.
- Target resolution assigns target_id = argmax(known_actor, estimated_signal_contribution); actors below LE awareness threshold are not targetable until an EvidenceToken creates awareness.

## Invariants
- InvestigatorMeter.current_level in [0.0, 1.0].
- InvestigatorMeter.fill_rate in [0.0, config.opsec.fill_rate_max].
- Status derivation order: `>= raid_threshold` -> raid_imminent; `>= formal_inquiry_threshold` -> formal_inquiry; `>= surveillance_threshold` -> surveillance; else inactive. Checked in descending threshold order.
- V1: one InvestigatorMeter per LE NPC. Simultaneous meters per LE NPC is expansion scope.
- LE NPCs filter to `criminal_sector == true` facilities for signal aggregation. Regulators read all facilities (criminal and legitimate). Journalists read public_info scope evidence only.
- NGO investigators cannot be neutralized by domestic political corruption; their fill_rate is not modified by `regional_corruption_coverage`.
- Regional signal formula: `regional_signal = sum(facility.signals.net_signal for all criminal_sector==true facilities in province) / config.opsec.facility_count_normalizer`.
- Fill rate formula: `fill_rate = clamp(regional_signal * config.opsec.detection_to_fill_rate_scale, 0.0, config.opsec.fill_rate_max)`.
- Floating-point accumulations for regional_signal use canonical sort order (facility_id ascending within province) for deterministic summation.
- Same seed + same inputs = identical investigator output regardless of core count.
- All random draws (raid delay, evidence discovery) through `DeterministicRNG`.

## Failure Modes
- No investigator NPC in province (all dead, fled, or imprisoned): no investigation activity for that province this tick. Criminal operations proceed unmonitored. Log warning.
- Evidence token referenced by meter no longer in pool (decayed or neutralized): meter recalculates without it; may cause fill_rate to drop.
- NaN or negative fill_rate from floating-point edge case: clamp to 0.0, log diagnostic.
- Target_id resolves to 0 (no known criminal actor): meter remains at inactive status with target_id = 0 sentinel. Fill rate still accrues from ambient signal; target assigned on first evidence token awareness.

## Performance Contract
- Province-parallel execution across up to 6 provinces.
- Target: < 15ms total for all investigator NPCs across 6 provinces (~50-100 LE NPCs total) on 6 cores.
- Per-investigator meter update: < 0.15ms average.
- Must not exceed tick budget share that would push total tick above 200ms target.

## Dependencies
- runs_after: ["criminal_operations", "facility_signals", "evidence"]
- runs_before: ["legal_process", "informant_system"]

## Test Scenarios
- `test_meter_fills_from_facility_signal`: A single criminal facility with net_signal 0.50 in a province with one LE NPC. Verify fill_rate = clamp(0.50 / 5.0 * 0.005, 0.0, 0.01) = 0.0005 per tick. Verify current_level increases by fill_rate.
- `test_surveillance_threshold_triggers_evidence`: LE NPC meter reaches 0.30. Verify status transitions to surveillance and a physical EvidenceToken is generated in the province.
- `test_formal_inquiry_opens_investigation`: LE NPC meter reaches 0.60. Verify status transitions to formal_inquiry and ConsequenceEntry(investigation_opens) is queued.
- `test_raid_imminent_schedules_consequence`: LE NPC meter reaches 0.80. Verify status transitions to raid_imminent and raid consequence is queued with delay 7-30 ticks.
- `test_corruption_reduces_fill_rate`: Province with corruption_susceptibility = 0.5 and regional_corruption_coverage = 0.8. Verify fill_rate is multiplied by (1.0 - 0.5 * 0.8) = 0.60.
- `test_target_resolution_argmax`: Two criminal actors in province with estimated signal contributions 0.3 and 0.7. Verify target_id resolves to the actor with 0.7 contribution.
- `test_meter_decays_when_signal_drops`: Criminal facility shuts down (net_signal = 0.0). Verify meter decays at config.investigator.decay_rate (0.001) per tick.
- `test_formal_inquiry_does_not_close_on_signal_drop`: LE NPC at formal_inquiry status with signal dropping to 0.0. Verify status remains formal_inquiry (does not revert to surveillance or inactive).
- `test_ngo_investigator_ignores_corruption`: NGO investigator NPC in province with high corruption coverage. Verify fill_rate is NOT modified by regional_corruption_coverage.
- `test_journalist_reads_public_evidence_only`: Journalist NPC in province with public_info and concealed evidence tokens. Verify journalist can only access public_info scope tokens for their investigation.
- `test_province_parallel_determinism`: Run 50 ticks of investigator processing with 6 provinces on 1 core and 6 cores. Verify bit-identical meter levels, statuses, and evidence token outputs.
- `test_violence_multiplier_on_personnel_violence`: Province with active TerritorialConflictStage at personnel_violence. Verify LE NPC fill_rate is multiplied by config.opsec.personnel_violence_multiplier (3.0).
